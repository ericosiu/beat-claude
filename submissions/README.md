# How to Submit

## Submission Process

1. **Pick a challenge** from [`/challenges`](../challenges/)
2. **Create your answer** (PDF or Markdown)
3. **Apply through our careers page:** **[singlegrain.com/careers](https://www.singlegrain.com/careers/)**
   Upload your challenge answer (PDF or Markdown) along with your application.

## What Happens Next

| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Submission received | Immediate confirmation |
| Blind review | Within 2 weeks |
| Results | Email notification |

## Rules

- **One submission per challenge** — no resubmissions
- **Use any tools you want** — AI, research, collaborators
- **No plagiarism** — your work, your thinking
- **Maximum 4 pages** — we value clarity and prioritization, not volume

## Questions?

- Open an issue in this repo
- Email `beat-claude@singlegrain.com`

## Evaluation

Your submission will be:
1. Anonymized
2. Scored alongside Claude's answer
3. Evaluated by Single Grain team members

See [SCORING.md](../SCORING.md) for the rubric.
