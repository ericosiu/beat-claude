# Challenge: SEO Strategy 003

## SEO Turnaround for a Declining Fintech Blog

### The Situation

You're advising a fintech company whose organic traffic has declined 40% over the past 12 months. Here's what you know:

**Company Snapshot**
- Series C fintech ($50M raised)
- Product: B2B expense management platform
- ARR: $30M
- Team: 1 Content Manager, 1 SEO Specialist, access to freelance writers
- Reports to: VP of Marketing

**Current SEO State**
- Domain Rating: 62 (Ahrefs)
- Organic traffic: 85K/mo (down from 140K/mo 12 months ago)
- Indexed pages: 800+
- Blog posts: 400+ articles (published over 5 years)
- Keywords ranking: 12,000 (down from 18,000)

**Traffic Breakdown**
- Blog: 70% of organic traffic
- Product pages: 15%
- Comparison pages: 10%
- Other: 5%

**Top Performing Content (historically)**
- "What is expense management" (was #1, now #4)
- "Business expense categories" (was #2, now #7)
- "Corporate card vs credit card" (was #3, now #5)
- Several posts have dropped off page 1 entirely

**Known Issues**
- Last content audit: 18 months ago
- Many posts are 3-5 years old with outdated information
- Site speed: 3.2s LCP (poor)
- Core Web Vitals: Failing on mobile
- Internal linking: Inconsistent
- Competitor Ramp published 50 new posts in past 6 months and is outranking on key terms

**Resources**
- Budget: $15K/mo for SEO (content, tools, contractors)
- Current tools: Ahrefs, Clearscope, WordPress
- Writers: 3 freelancers at $300/post

### Your Task

Create a **12-month SEO recovery plan** to get back to 140K organic traffic/mo and improve conversion from organic visitors.

### What to Submit

Your plan should include:

1. **Diagnostic Analysis**
   - What's causing the traffic decline?
   - How do you prioritize what to fix?
   - What data would you want access to?

2. **Content Strategy**
   - What do you do with 400+ existing posts?
   - What new content should you create?
   - How do you prioritize refresh vs. new?

3. **Technical SEO**
   - How do you address Core Web Vitals?
   - What technical fixes are highest priority?
   - How do you improve crawl efficiency?

4. **Competitive Response**
   - How do you respond to Ramp's content push?
   - What's your differentiation strategy?
   - Which battles do you fight vs. avoid?

5. **Link Building**
   - What's your approach with a $15K budget?
   - How do you build authority in fintech?
   - Realistic link acquisition targets?

6. **12-Month Roadmap**
   - Quarter-by-quarter priorities
   - Key milestones and metrics
   - Team/resource allocation

### Constraints

- Cannot hire full-time for 6 months
- Engineering resources limited (1 sprint/quarter for SEO fixes)
- Must maintain publishing cadence (4 posts/month minimum)
- Leadership wants to see traction in 90 days
