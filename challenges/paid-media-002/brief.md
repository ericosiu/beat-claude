# Challenge: Paid Media 002

## Scaling a B2B SaaS from $50K to $200K/mo in Paid Spend

### The Situation

You're advising a Series B B2B SaaS company that needs to scale paid acquisition. Here's what you know:

**Company Snapshot**
- ARR: $8M (growing 80% YoY)
- Raised $25M Series B (12 months ago)
- Team: 1 Paid Media Manager, 1 Designer, access to freelance copywriters
- Reports to: VP of Marketing

**Current Paid Performance**
- Monthly spend: $50K
- Channels: Google Ads (60%), LinkedIn (30%), Meta (10%)
- Blended CAC: $1,200
- Target CAC: $1,500 (has room to increase)
- Average deal size: $18K ACV
- Sales cycle: 45 days from MQL to closed-won

**Funnel Metrics**
- Demo requests/month: 180
- Demo → Opportunity: 40%
- Opportunity → Closed Won: 25%
- Net new customers/month: 18

**Product**
- Project management tool for construction companies
- Mid-market focus (50-500 employees)
- Key differentiator: AI-powered schedule optimization
- Integrates with Procore, Autodesk, and other construction software

**Market Context**
- TAM: ~15,000 construction companies in target segment (US)
- 3 main competitors with larger marketing budgets
- Seasonal: Q1 and Q4 are strongest (project planning cycles)

### Your Task

Create a **paid media scaling plan** to go from $50K/mo to $200K/mo spend over 6 months while maintaining or improving CAC efficiency.

### What to Submit

Your plan should include:

1. **Channel Strategy**
   - How do you allocate the increased budget across channels?
   - What new channels (if any) would you test?
   - What's your rationale for each allocation?

2. **Campaign Architecture**
   - What campaign types and structures for each channel?
   - How do you segment audiences?
   - What's your creative strategy?

3. **Scaling Roadmap**
   - Month-by-month spend progression
   - Key milestones and decision points
   - When do you add headcount vs. agency vs. tools?

4. **Measurement Framework**
   - What metrics do you track at each stage of the funnel?
   - How do you attribute revenue to paid channels?
   - What's your testing methodology?

5. **Risk Mitigation**
   - What could cause CAC to blow up as you scale?
   - How do you maintain creative freshness?
   - What's your plan if LinkedIn CPCs spike?

6. **Budget Breakdown**
   - Detailed allocation: media spend, tools, creative, headcount
   - Expected CAC at $100K, $150K, and $200K spend levels

### Constraints

- Current team is maxed out at $50K spend
- Leadership expects CAC to stay under $1,500
- Cannot hire full-time for 90 days (budget freeze)
- Must maintain brand guidelines (no aggressive/salesy creative)
